Harry Potter Book Review

Harry Potter and the Sorcerers Stone, is the first book in the many books in the Harry Potter series, created by J. K. Rowling. It is the first book I have read of this series, and I give it a 10/10

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets. This book is filled with courage, friendship, and love. It is the perfect mix for those who love fantasy books. 10/10

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban is about Harry meeting his uncle, it is an amazing story and I recommend you read it. 10/10

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire is another amazing book in the Harry Potter series. It introduces a new side of the world of magic, by having a giant tournament of magic, and the quidditch world cup. 10/10

Harry Potter the Order of the Phoenix tells the story about how the students in Hogwarts fight against the forces of evil when the ministry of magic will not. 10/10
